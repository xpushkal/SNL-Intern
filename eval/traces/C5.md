### Turn 2

**User**

> What’s the difference between OPQ and OPQ MQ Sales Report?

**Agent**

**OPQ (OPQ32r)** is the underlying **personality questionnaire**: a broad, standard measure of workplace behavioural style used across roles and decisions (development, team fit, etc.).

**OPQ MQ Sales Report** is a **reporting product**, not a different questionnaire. It summarizes OPQ results in a **sales-specific** way—graphical and narrative emphasis on behaviours tied to sales success. You can **optionally** add the **Motivation Questionnaire (MQ)** so the same report also reflects **sales motivators and drives**; without MQ, you still get the sales-framed OPQ story.

So: one assessment instrument (OPQ32r) for personality; the Sales Report is how you **read** those results for sellers (and optionally enrich with MQ).
